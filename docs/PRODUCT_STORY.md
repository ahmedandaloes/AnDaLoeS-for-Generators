## The Story

**AnDaLoeS is the trust-based booking marketplace for generator rental in
Egypt** — built for events, construction sites, factories, telecom towers, and
farms that need reliable power on a schedule, not blackout panic. Today owners
hide behind phone numbers on classifieds (no rates, no calendar, no proof of who
they are) while rental firms quote opaquely and chase only mega-jobs. We close
that gap: every listing shows a **published day-rate, books in one tap, and is
backed by a verified owner rated by real renters.** Our promise is simple — *see
the price, book in one tap, and the generator shows up on time.* We win on the
whitespace no incumbent fills: **transparent pricing + a real trust-and-
fulfillment layer**, sized for the SMB/B2B jobs driving Egypt's growing rental
demand. North star: make it **effortless for both sides** and get every
rental fulfilled **on time**.

## Market Challenges (and our answer)

| Challenge | Our answer |
|---|---|
| **Grid stabilized** — consumer outage demand fading | Anchor on durable **B2B/SMB** (events, construction, factories, telecom, agriculture); grid instability is upside, not the bet |
| **Cash market (~55%)** — can't auto-collect commission | **Commission ledger now** (accrue + mark-collected), then Fawry/escrow auto-collect; plus leakage-proof owner subscription/featured fees |
| **Incumbents** — OLX classifieds (no trust/booking) + phone-only opaque firms | **Published rates + one-tap verified booking + reviews** — the thing none of them do |
| **Two-sided cold start** | City-by-city seeding (Cairo + Alexandria first) with a supply-liquidity tracker |
| **Trust gap** in a high-value physical rental | Verified owners, deposits, reliability scores, dispute flow |
| **Seasonality** (summer events/festival peaks) | Pre-position both sides before peak; recurring re-book loop for telecom/agri |
| **Regulation & tax** (like Uber) | Tax-ready invoicing (VAT on commission, tax #), accounting exports, ETA e-invoicing + CBE compliance as gated [LATER] items — *with professional advice* |

## How We Win (one line)
Be the **easiest, most transparent, most trustworthy** way to rent a generator
in Egypt — and make sure it **arrives on time** — while quietly building the
revenue and compliance rails to scale.
